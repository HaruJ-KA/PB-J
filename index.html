Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Sauce
  • Meat (Ground Beef) - 1 Pound
  • Garlic - 4 Cloves
  • Oregano - 2 Teaspoons
  • Salt - 1 Teaspoon
  • Tomato (Diced) - 28 Ounces

Instructions

  1. Cook ground beef until browned and crumbly.
  2. Add in sauce and diced tomatoes, add in Oregano and Salt, let simmer for an hour.
  3. Boil spaghetti to enjoyed degree.
  4. Put sauce onto spaghetti.
  5. Enjoy spaghetti.
